A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi there, my name is Martine McGrath. As a designer I enjoy discovering, learning and most importantly crafting engaging designs and experiences.

I am currently studying Interaction Design at University of Ulster, Belfast, exploring the world of design and tackling the challenges that come with it. I have always had a passion for art and design, more so when I started my journey as an IXD student. Throughout my course I have developed a extensive design knowledge and skills. It has opened my mind to many design practices allowing me understand every aspect of design. Design for me is a journey of discovery, allowing me to explore endless possibilities and continue learning new techniques everyday.

For any project (big or small) I will always follow these three main stages whilst developing an idea and gradually progress from paper to pixel. This process allows to me explore all possible ideas, break down any project into manageable sections, and produce successful outcomes.

PIXEL

Next, is where the magic begins. Using whatever application suitable I take potential ideas from the paper explorations and create a digital outcome.

PAPER

Paper prototyping is always the first step of my design process. This is where all ideas generate and I can quickly transfer ideas into something visual.

PERFECTING

Finally comes the perfecting stage. Refinements and adjustments are made to the design, allowing me to pay attention to detail and ensures the design is perfect.
