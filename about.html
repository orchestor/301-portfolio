A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi there, i'm Martine, A petite, perfectionist, pizza loving illustrator and interaction designer. As a designer I enjoy discovering, learning and most importantly crafting engaging designs and experiences.

I am currently studying Interaction Design at Belfast school of Art, exploring the world of design and tackling the challenges that come with it. I have always had a passion for art and design, more so when I started my journey as an IXD student. Throughout my course I have developed a extensive design knowledge and skills. It has opened my mind to many design practices allowing me understand every aspect of design. Design for me is a journey of discovery, allowing me to explore endless possibilities and continue learning new techniques everyday.

MY SKILLS

For any project (big or small) I will always gradually progress from paper to pixel. Paper is my starting point, then I move to pixel where the magic begins and finally perfecting the design. My experience in design has resulted in me experimenting in a wide range of design areas and developing a number of skills.
