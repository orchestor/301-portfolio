MASTER & APPRENTICE

A number of illustration master & apprentice exercises to continue improving my illustration skills and learning new techniques.

BRIEF

The term master-apprentice is a method of learning the involves recreating a design that already exsists. The aim of this process is that the apprentice re-makes the works of the master, eventually seeing and thinking like the master. These exercises allows the apprentice to learn new skills, improve current skills and therefore succeed.

APPROACH

When completing these tasks I always look for something that will challange me. When re-making work of whoever I choose I break down the illustrations and examine which tools are required to make it quickly and accurately. I really enjoy completing these task and feel they ensure I am constantly learning and improving my skills. These exercises then also lead to inspiration for my own illustrations.
